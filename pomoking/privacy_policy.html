Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Thirteensoft ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in relation to the mobile application PomoKing ("App")[cite: 1].

1. Overview

PomoKing is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require user accounts and we do not directly collect personally identifiable information[cite: 1].

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store names, email addresses, location data, or personal identifiers[cite: 1].

3. Data Stored Locally

The App stores Pomodoro session history, usage statistics, and preferences locally on your device[cite: 1].

4. Cloud Backup

On supported platforms, your data may be backed up using Google Drive (Android) or Apple iCloud (iOS)[cite: 1]. This data is controlled by your personal account; we do not access it[cite: 1].

5. Third-Party Services

a) RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases[cite: 1]. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase data and device identifiers[cite: 1].

b) Sentry

We use Sentry for error monitoring, which may include crash logs and device type[cite: 1]. This is used strictly for app stability[cite: 1].

6. Purchases

All payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play[cite: 1]. We do not store or process payment information[cite: 1].

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data[cite: 1].

8. Data Retention

Data retention is controlled entirely by the user and their device backups[cite: 1].

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data[cite: 1].

10. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Under the GDPR (EU) and CCPA (USA), users have rights including the right to access, rectify, or erase their data. Because we do not store personal data on our servers, you may exercise these rights by:

  • Deleting your data locally within the App.
  • Managing your backups in iCloud or Google Drive.
  • Contacting our third-party partners (RevenueCat/Sentry) regarding device-specific identifiers.

11. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date[cite: 1].
