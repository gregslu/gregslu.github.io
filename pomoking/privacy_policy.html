Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Thirteensoft ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PomoKing mobile application. This policy details how we handle user data in compliance with the latest 2026 App Store and Google Play requirements.

1. Data Collection & Usage

PomoKing is designed to help you focus. To provide our features, we collect:

  • Usage Statistics: Timer durations, focus sessions, and category tags to generate your productivity insights.
  • Transaction Information: We use RevenueCat to manage Monthly, Annual, and Lifetime subscriptions. We do not store your credit card details; these are securely handled by Apple/Google.
  • Device Information: Hardware model and OS version to ensure app compatibility and performance.

2. Third-Party Integrations

We share limited data with the following essential services:

  • RevenueCat: To validate receipts and manage your premium subscription status across devices.
  • Firebase Analytics: To monitor app performance and crash reports (data is anonymized where possible).

3. Mandatory Account Deletion

In accordance with 2026 platform mandates, you have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated focus history directly within the PomoKing app settings. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
